    [PT-BR]Cryptocurrencies: are these viable forms of money?
    Institute of Economics at Federal University of Rio de Janeiro
    The present work aims to present an introduction to the study of cryptocurrencies, focusing on Bitcoin, the first cryptocurrency created.     [PT-BR]Cryptocurrencies – Viable Forms Of Money? A Brief Analysis Of The Characteristics And Functions Of Currency Compared To Electronic Currencies
    [PT-BR]VIII Federal University of Rio de Janeiro Academic Integration Week
    In recent years, technological advancements have significantly impacted society, exemplified by the proliferation of smartphones and the emergence of electronic currencies, with Bitcoin being the most prominent.   • 2012.10.02
    [PT-BR]Entrepreneurial Attitude Paradigms: An Analysis of the Elements that Facilitate the Creation of New ICT Businesses in Brazil
    [PT-BR]XXXIV Giulio Massarani Journey of Scientific, Artistic and Cultural Initiation - Federal University of Rio de Janeiro
    In the evolving landscape of the post-Fordist era and the rise of the knowledge society, the Brazilian technology, information, and communication (TIC) sector is witnessing growth opportunities fueled by an expanding middle class and increasing digital inclusion. This study delves into the factors influencing the creation of new businesses within the Brazilian TIC sector, shedding light on the pivotal role of individual entrepreneurial traits, socio-cultural context, and access to information and knowledge, while also revealing challenges in access to funding, internationalization, and government support. With insights from 76 valid responses, this research underscores the significance of individual attributes in the entrepreneurial decision-making process, ultimately presenting a comprehensive analysis of the sector's dynamics.
